# Executive Summary – FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard

Developed an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard for FNP (Ferns N Petals) using Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product trends, and delivery efficiency. The objective of this project was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that support better decision-making and performance tracking.

The dashboard provides a complete overview of business KPIs, including Total Orders (1000), Total Revenue (3.52M+), Average Delivery Time (5.53 days), and Average Customer Spend (3520.98). Advanced Excel features such as Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, Conditional Formatting, and Dynamic Visualizations were used to create an interactive and user-friendly reporting solution.

**Key business insights identified during the analysis include:**

* Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan generated the highest revenue among all occasions, highlighting strong seasonal demand.
  
* Colors, Soft Toys, and Sweets were the highest-performing product categories contributing significantly to overall sales.
  
* Revenue trends showed noticeable spikes during February and August, indicating increased customer spending during festive and special occasions.
  
* Order activity peaked during evening hours, helping identify high customer engagement periods.
  
* Top-performing cities consistently contributed higher revenue, enabling location-based performance analysis.
  
* Products such as Magnum Set and Quia Gift emerged as top-selling products with strong customer demand.

The dashboard includes interactive filters for Order Date, Delivery Date, and Occasion, allowing users to dynamically explore trends, compare performance metrics, and perform drill-down analysis in real time.
